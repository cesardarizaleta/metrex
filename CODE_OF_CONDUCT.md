# Community Code of Conduct

We are committed to a friendly, safe and welcoming environment for all.

- Be respectful and inclusive. Discrimination or harassment is not tolerated.
- Assume good faith and prefer collaboration over conflict.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and technical topics.
- Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainers.

Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings or removal from the community, to enforce this code of conduct.
